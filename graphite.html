About Artist

Tenzing was born and raised in Kathmandu, Nepal. Some hundred miles from the himalayas. His art is mostly about the people and the places that he encounters in daily life. These people and places inspire him to explore art in different ways to express different human emotions using different mediums. These graphite show the people as individuals and a part of themself without objectification. It gives a glimpse of all the wondeful things they have.
